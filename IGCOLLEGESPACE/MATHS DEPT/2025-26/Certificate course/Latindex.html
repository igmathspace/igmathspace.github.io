About the Course

Course Overview

CC25MAT — LaTeXing with Overleaf is a comprehensive certificate course designed to equip students with professional documentation skills using LaTeX and Overleaf. The course covers everything from basic document structure to advanced mathematical typesetting, enabling learners to create high-quality academic and research documents.

Course Content

Module I : Foundations (6 hrs)
Overleaf interface · Hello World · Document hierarchy · Text formatting · Lists
Module II : Page Design (6 hrs)
Front matter (maketitle) · Headers · Multi-column layouts · Research formatting
Module III : Tables & Graphics (8 hrs)
Grids & merging · booktabs borders · Images (graphicx) · Captions & referencing
Module IV : Math Typesetting (10 hrs)
Inline/display math · Greek letters & operators · Multi-line alignment · Matrices & theorems

Course Objectives

  • Master the Overleaf interface and LaTeX document structure
  • Create professional academic documents with proper formatting
  • Design sophisticated tables, graphics, and multi-column layouts
  • Typeset complex mathematical expressions, matrices, and theorems
  • Prepare research papers and documentation with LaTeX standards
